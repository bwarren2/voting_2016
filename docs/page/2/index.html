Ohio

Let’s start with Ohio. The outliers of Ohio are mostly concentrated in the east and southeast parts of the state. Change in Republican Presidential vote percentage points, 2016-2012 What is notable about that part of the state? That is where the coal seams are, and where coal mining has historically happened. That outlier area ~= coal country An initial guess might be that coal miners had exceptionally negative views of Hillary Clinton, and/or positive views of Donald Trump.
